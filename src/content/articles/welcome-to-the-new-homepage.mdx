For more than a decade, this league has lived in scattered group chats, the occasional spreadsheet, and the dark recesses of Yahoo Fantasy. **No more.** Welcome to the new home of **Greybushes & Chili Dogs**.

## What's here now

- **Standings** that update straight from Yahoo every fifteen minutes.
- **Matchups** with live scores and projections, plus a week selector for nostalgia trips.
- **Teams** with full rosters, because we all want to know exactly who is starting Pat Freiermuth.
- **Draft** board so you can re-litigate that 4th-round disaster.
- **Transactions** feed for the FAAB micromanagers.
- **Articles** like this one.
- **Members** roster, **History**, **Payouts**, and the **Wall of Shame**.

## What's next

The wall of shame is *empty*. That cannot stand. If you have receipts: punishments served, glorious championships, infamous trades, send them to the commissioner. We're rebuilding the institutional memory one hot take at a time.

> "A bunch of degenerates who claim to be extraordinary swindlers."

Stay tuned.
